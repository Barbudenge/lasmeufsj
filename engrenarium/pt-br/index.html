Sobre o Engrenarium

O Engrenarium ajuda estudantes, pesquisadores e engenheiros a explorar trens de engrenagens planetárias, cinemática e projeto de transmissões com software de visualização interativa.

  • Teaching planetary gear systems Allows visual demonstration of operating principles and kinematic relationships in planetary systems.
  • Visualization of complex mechanisms Makes it easier to understand the interaction between sun, planets, and ring gear in different configurations.
  • Exploration of design configurations Makes it possible to quickly test different gear arrangements and parameters.
  • Preliminary transmission analysis Helps with the initial evaluation of planetary transmission architectures.
  • Simulation of multiple planetary stages Allows the study of systems with several planetary sets in series.
  • Generation of models and technical files Allows exporting geometric representations for use in documentation or other software.
  • Research support tool Can be used in the investigation and analysis of gear configurations in academic projects.
  • Support for documenting laboratory projects Helps record and illustrate mechanisms studied in technical and academic activities.

Licensing

Licenses and pricing

Engrenarium is available in Free and Pro versions. The Free version covers essential usage, while the Pro version adds advanced capabilities, broader deployment options, and licensing plans for individuals and institutions.

What the Pro version adds

  • Export of 3D models in STL and DXF formats.
  • Export of simulation videos in AVI and WebM formats.
  • Ability to save projects.
  • Ability to store multiple operating conditions within a project (e.g., different gear states or gear ratios).
  • Support for multiple selectable gear ratios (Free version allows only one).
  • Ability to define independent gear parameters for each planetary stage.
  • Support for an unlimited number of planetary stages in a model (Free version allows up to two).
  • Support for an unlimited number of planet gears per planetary stage (Free version allows up to two planets per stage).
  • Ability to modify fundamental gear geometry parameters, including module (diametral pitch), pressure angle, and helix angle.

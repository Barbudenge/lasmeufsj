Engrenarium

Engrenarium is a LASME project focused on the simulation and visualization of planetary gear systems in a web environment.

Open simulator Back to LASME

Summary

A tool under development to support teaching activities, demonstrations, and exploration of mechanical configurations.

About the project

This page presents an initial overview of Engrenarium. The goal is to gather information about the project proposal, its context within LASME, and the materials that will be published later.

Initial applications

Teaching

Support for classes and demonstrations about kinematic relations, gear configurations, and the behavior of planetary systems.

Exploration

A space to test different arrangements and visually observe how the components relate inside the mechanism.

Documentation

A base to consolidate project descriptions, usage references, and supporting materials produced by the laboratory.

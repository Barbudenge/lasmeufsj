About the laboratory

LASME carries out teaching and research activities in mechanical systems, including modeling and simulation, development of didactic tools, and analysis of mechanisms and transmissions. The laboratory also maintains software projects and supporting materials for mechanical engineering courses.

Research and development focus

Mechanisms and kinematics

Analysis and synthesis of mechanisms, motion relationships, and system configurations.

Transmissions and gears

Planetary systems and gear train combinations with a didactic and simulation-oriented emphasis.

Computational tools

Web applications and utilities for visualization, documentation, and teaching support.
